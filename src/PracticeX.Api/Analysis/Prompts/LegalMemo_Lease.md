## Real Estate (Lease / Lease Amendment / LOI) — Issue Overlay

Run through this checklist in addition to the master register. Surface
each item that is unfavorable, ambiguous, or missing.

### Economic terms
- **Base rent escalators** — fixed % vs CPI vs market reset; cap on CPI
  escalators ("CPI not to exceed 3%"); compounding vs simple.
- **Operating expense pass-throughs** — gross / NN / NNN; controllable vs
  uncontrollable opex; caps on controllable opex (5%/year is typical);
  exclusions list (ground rent, mortgage, leasing commissions, capital
  repairs over X years); audit rights for tenant on landlord's books.
- **Real estate taxes** — gross-up provisions; tenant share of any
  reassessment from sale of building (Prop 13 traps in California; in
  NC/most states no statutory limit so a sale can spike taxes).
- **Tenant improvement allowance** — $/sqft amount; deadline to use;
  unused-amount treatment (forfeit vs rent credit); landlord-controlled
  construction vs tenant-controlled.
- **Free rent / abatement** — months and conditions; recapture on default.
- **Security deposit / letter of credit** — amount; burn-down schedule;
  L/C issuer requirements; replenishment obligations.

### Use, exclusivity, and operational
- **Permitted use** — narrow vs broad; specialty-medical exclusivity
  (does the lease grant US the exclusive right to operate a [specialty]
  practice in the building / center?); does the landlord retain the
  right to lease to a competitor?
- **Hours of operation / continuous-operation covenant** — material
  obligation if the practice ever consolidates locations.
- **Signage rights** — building, monument, suite; landlord approval
  standards.
- **Parking** — number of spaces, dedicated vs shared, charge per space,
  patient/visitor parking; healthcare practices need patient flow.

### Term, options, and exit
- **Initial term** — and the math: when does notice for renewal open and
  close?
- **Renewal option** — fixed term or "fair market rent"? FMR with what
  process (appraisal, arbitration, baseball arbitration)? FMR floor at
  prior rent?
- **Early termination** — termination-for-convenience right? termination
  fee / unamortized TI buyout?
- **Holdover rent** — 150% / 200% of base rent is common; 200%+ with
  consequential damages is harsh; is it cumulative with consequential?
- **Assignment / subletting** — landlord consent standard ("not to be
  unreasonably withheld"?); recapture right; profit-sharing on sublease;
  permitted-transfer carve-out for affiliates / change-of-control / sale
  of business is critical for a medical practice (sale to a hospital
  system or PE rollup must not require landlord consent).

### Compliance and risk
- **Maintenance and repair allocation** — structural vs non-structural;
  HVAC and roof are common landlord-vs-tenant battles.
- **Compliance with laws** — who pays for ADA / OSHA / building-code
  upgrades triggered by tenant's specific use vs general code changes?
- **Hazardous materials** — pre-existing vs tenant-caused; a medical
  tenant always brings biohazard / sharps / radioactive (if imaging) —
  tenant indemnity scope and landlord representation about pre-existing.
- **Insurance** — limits ($1M/$2M general liability is light for a
  medical practice; $2M/$5M is standard); waiver of subrogation between
  landlord and tenant; landlord as additional insured.
- **Indemnity** — mutual? carve-outs for landlord's gross negligence?
  cap?
- **Casualty and condemnation** — termination thresholds; rent abatement
  during repair; who controls insurance proceeds.
- **SNDA (subordination, non-disturbance, attornment)** — does tenant
  have a right to a non-disturbance agreement from the landlord's
  lender? Without it, foreclosure can wipe out the lease.
- **Estoppel certificate** — turnaround time; cap on what tenant has to
  certify.

### Healthcare-specific
- **Stark/AKS** — if the landlord is a referring physician or referral
  source, the rent must be FMV and not vary with referrals; flag any
  rent or term linked to volume.
- **HIPAA** — landlord access provisions; common-area cleaning — does
  the landlord's vendor have access to areas where PHI is stored?

### Amendment-specific (if this is a lease amendment)
- **What is the amendment changing?** Quote the exact prior language and
  the new language for each modified clause.
- **What is being silently preserved?** An amendment that doesn't touch
  a bad provision in the master lease *ratifies* that provision.
- **Cumulative effect** — does the amendment, combined with prior
  amendments, change the economic deal or risk allocation in a way the
  partners haven't tracked?
